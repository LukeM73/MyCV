About Me

Being born in South Africa and raised in both Cape Town and Plettenberg Bay, I have experienced two distinct sides of South African life. The "work hard and dream big" mentality of the city contrasts with the "work together and build together" approach of small-town life. These perspectives have shaped my worldview and built the foundation for my dreams and aspirations.

I am passionate about solving problems and finding innovative solutions to challenges. With an immense fascination for technology and science, I'm constantly driven to understand how things work and how to improve them. The more complex the problem, the more motivated I am to solve it!

My engineering passion was ignited during a matric job shadowing experience at HPE in Johannesburg, a company that designs and produces mining drills. The design and testing phases captivated me - creating solutions to improve existing systems was incredibly rewarding.
